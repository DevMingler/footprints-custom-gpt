# Footprints Custom GPT

A reflective GPT that helps people explore the paths they have walked through their conversations.

Upload a ChatGPT export and Footprints will identify recurring themes, projects, interests, questions, and patterns that emerged over time.

The goal is not analytics.

The goal is reflection.

---

## What is Footprints?

Footprints is designed to help users better understand their own conversation history.

Given a ChatGPT export (`chat.json`), Footprints attempts to surface:

* Major projects
* Recurring themes
* Persistent questions
* Creative and reflective seasons
* Emerging interests
* Turning points and shifts in focus
* Patterns that may not be obvious when viewed conversation by conversation

Rather than focusing on individual messages, Footprints looks for the larger story that emerges across many conversations.

---

## How It Works

1. The user exports their ChatGPT history.
2. The user uploads the export to Footprints.
3. Footprints analyses the conversation history.
4. A reflective report is generated.

The report focuses on patterns, themes, and long-term trends rather than isolated conversations.

---

## Limitations

Footprints is an experimental reflection tool.

Outputs are generated from conversation history and may:

* Miss important context
* Misinterpret intent
* Overlook significant events
* Reflect incomplete information

Reports should be treated as reflective observations rather than objective truth.

Users should apply their own judgment when interpreting results.
